At Witz End

Keep your Witz about ya!

Finding herself stressed out and overworked, this is one New Yorker's journey on a quest to learn work/life balance. Such as:

  • cooking without setting off the fire alarm
  • what is this thing called "free time"
  • the power of sleeping

This is my personal blog as I ebmark on a journey from overworked manhattan commuter to Denver out-doors-liking-person. In the process I've begun cooking, paused cooking while my belongings got stuck on a moving truck for many weeks, then resumed cooking again. Cardio is hard this close to the mountains, but eventually I'll attempt that as well. I was at the end of my rope and losing my wits, but I'll always be Jwitz: the terrible cook. Well.. hopefully eventually the not-so-terrible cook. At least Jwitz: the can feed people
